# Tiered Futures Breakout Skill
# On MORNING_SPIKE, wait for 5-min consolidation breakout.
# Tier 1: Enter 0.5% of Striker pool.
# Breakeven: Stop moves to entry when profit >= 1.5x initial risk.
# Tier 2: After TP1 hit, add position of equal size, stop at breakeven.
# Tier 3: Repeat once more if trend continues.
# Trailing stop: 1 ATR below highest price since entry.
# Total loss across all tiers never exceeds 0.5% of Striker pool.
# Instrument: Perpetual futures on BTC/USDT or SOL/USDT.
